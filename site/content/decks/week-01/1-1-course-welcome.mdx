<Slide title="Projects" key="Deliverables are short, audience-aware slide decks — just like a real analyst's">

- 4 projects throughout the course
  - 3 pre-determined, 1 choose your own adventure
- Deliverable will be short slide-deck
  - Focus on communicating conclusion
  - Your audience will be clearly defined
  - Intended to simulate what a DA would do

</Slide>

<Slide title="How You're Graded" key="Projects and homework carry the most weight — applied work matters most">

- **Homework — 30%:** self-guided practice of each week’s topics
- **Projects 1–3 — 30%:** the three pre-determined analyst deliverables
- **Labs — 20%:** guided, in-class application
- **Project 4 — 20%:** the choose-your-own-adventure capstone

</Slide>

<Slide title="Healthy Skepticism" key="Analytics augments professional judgment — it doesn't replace it">

  - Question the assumptions
  - Question the model
  - Question the data
