<Slide title="Why Automate">

- Save time and money
  - Your time!
- Avoid errors
- Repeatable processes (auditable)
- Planning promotes robust solutions

</Slide>

<Slide title="Automating Data Preparation – ETL">

- ETL is a process to prepare data for use
- Extract – collecting data, often from myriad sources
- Transform – merge and clean the data
- Load – save consistent, analyzable format to storage

</Slide>

<Slide title="Automating Data Use">

- Dashboards, KPIs for decision makers
- Continual process analysis, internal audit
- Robotic Process Automation (RPA) to achieve specific tasks
- Small one-off tasks (filing PDFs, scraping data, etc.)

</Slide>

<Slide title="Automation Caveats">

- Unknown unknowns will always occur
  - Make sure to handle them – error checking and reporting
- Automation development is an iterative process
  - Try, break, fix, repeat
- People writing code may not be accountants
  - Domain expertise is imperative to understanding what assumptions to make, check their work!

</Slide>
