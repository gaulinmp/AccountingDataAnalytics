<Slide title="Audit">

- Audits used to random sample to draw inferences
- Now automated analysis of “all” data
- Auditor now focuses on understanding the models, parameters, assumptions, and outputs
  - Design classifiers for flagging suspicious transactions
  - Adjust RPA bot parameters to balance risk and cost

</Slide>

<Slide title="Invoice Processing">

- Invoice received via email with multiple attachments
- OCR processing to extract data, AI to structure text
  - Find invoice date, numbers, amounts, due date
  - Extract all invoice line items across all pages, check items against purchase order
- Route data based on confidence in extracted data
  - Reconciliation queue to auto-pay for good extraction
  - Manual review for bad extraction
- Generate daily report for all invoices and bot results

</Slide>

<Slide title="Bank Reconciliation">

- Bank statements report actual cash transference
- Needs matching against cash ledger and journal entries
- RPA bot for each bank portal (due to their significant differences)
- Pass off data to aggregation and reconciliation task
- Flag mismatches and large unexpected transfers

</Slide>
