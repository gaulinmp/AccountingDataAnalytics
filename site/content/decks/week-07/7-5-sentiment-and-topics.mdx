<Slide title="Topics and Sentiment">

- Two common NLP tasks
  - What is the text about?
  - Is it good or bad news?
- Both often compliment quantitative information
- Both are “dimensionality reduction” approaches

</Slide>

<Slide title="Sentiment Analysis">

- Classifies text into positive, negative, neutral
- Old method: rule or dictionary based
  - Look for negative or positive words
- New method: statistically based
  - Requires examples for training
  - Off the shelf generic LLM solution

</Slide>

<Slide title="Sentiment in Accounting">

- Analyzing competitors disclosures
- Reviewing investor sentiment on conference calls
- Demand estimation from customer responses/posts
- Identifying early risk indicators from social media

</Slide>

<Slide title="Topic Modeling">

- Categorize content of text into topics
- Customized to content and desired topics
- Models:
  - LDA: define number of topics, requires lots of data to work well
  - NMF: better for shorter texts and less data
  - BERTopic: more advanced, single topic per text

</Slide>
