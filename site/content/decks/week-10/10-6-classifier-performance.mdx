<Slide title="Classifier asymmetric Costs">

- TP / TN – true, so no costs
- False Positive – expect bankruptcy, firm remains a going concern
  - Waste of resources expended to mitigate risk
- False Negative – expect going concern, firm liquidates
  - Up to complete loss
