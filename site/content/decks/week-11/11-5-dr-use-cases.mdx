<Slide title="Examples of Dimensionality Reduction">

- Precursor to classifiers or clustering
- Visualization of high dimensional data
- Textual Analysis – topic analysis, embeddings

</Slide>

<Slide title="Precursor to further analytics">

- Some models based on distance (e.g. KNN) perform more poorly as number of features increases
- Applying dimensionality reduction first limits the dimensions to the most informative combinations
- Makes calculations possible
- Lessens risk of over-fitting

</Slide>

<Slide title="Visualization of myriad variables">

- The interactions between variables is easy to observe “pair wise” in a pair plot, but not 3D+
- PCA, t-SNE, UMAP, etc. preserve “structure” in the dataset, allowing for visual observation of clusters and other relationships in 2D that represent 3D+

</Slide>
